ORGANIZATIONS

These are only two of several important organizations that have made important impacts on society. Checking out their websites will give you some clues about how else to get involved!

Climate Action Campaign

The Climate Action Campaign is a local San Diego organization, started by politician Nicole Capretz. She's the reason behind the adoption of eight 100% clean energy plans, and is trying to make a bigger impact across all of SoCal.

Go to Website Contact Them

Fridays for Future

Fridays for Future is a global organization, started by activist Greta Thunberg. Her strikes gave her worldwide recognition, which she's used to bring more attention to the problem of climate change.
